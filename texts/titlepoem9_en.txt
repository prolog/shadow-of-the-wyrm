Over hill, over dale, %b
thorough brush, thorough brier, %b
Over park, over pale, %b
Thorough flood, thorough fire, %b
I do wander everywhere, %b
Swifter than the moon's sphere; %b
And I serve the fairy queen, %b
to dew her orbs upon the green.
%p
- From "A Midsummer Night's Dream", William Shakespeare
